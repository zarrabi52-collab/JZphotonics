About JZ Photonics

Principal consultant: Joseph H. Zarrabi, Ph.D.

Dr. Joseph Zarrabi is a senior optical engineer and laser scientist based in San Diego, California. His work spans kW-class fiber lasers, satellite laser communication terminals, SWIR imaging systems, optical diagnostics, high-power laser integration, Zemax modeling, tolerance analysis, and hands-on optical metrology.

Consulting Services

Program Technical Leadership
